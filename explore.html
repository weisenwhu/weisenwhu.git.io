Nanjing

Ancient capital of Six Dynasties

Nanjing has a prominent place in Chinese history and culture, having been the capital of China for several periods. Located in the lower Yangtze River drainage basin and Yangtze River Delta economic zone, Nanjing has long been one of China's most important cities.Having been the capital city of six different dynasties since 3 A.D., it is recognized as one of the Four Great Ancient Capitals of China.
